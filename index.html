About Me

Hello! My name is Nate Pond, a young professional who is currently studying coding at UNC. My background is in community relations from my experience as a Community Relations Specialist for FEMA to my experience as a Field Manager during the election for Working America AFLCIO.

I have a large variety of interests on topics raning from skepticism, ethics in journalism, Philosophy, Horror, Weird fiction, to Cyberpunk. I play guitar. I enjoy Munchkins.

Someday I would like to combine my knowledge of the anthropology of economics, media theory, and coding for the purpose of social engineering and facilitating localization.
